Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my personal portfolio website is to showcase my expertise in rebranding and attract potential clients. It aims to achieve this by presenting a collection of successful rebranding projects, providing valuable content for my target audience to understand my message and approach, and creating a visually appealing and easily readable website that reflects my branding and style.

Audience

The main target of my services are businesses, organizations, and individuals interested in rebranding. This includes small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) looking to refresh their brand, startups aiming to establish a unique brand identity, established companies going through repositioning or expansion, non-profit organizations seeking to improve their brand perception, and entrepreneurs launching new products or services.

Page 4 - Portfolio

Portfolio 1: Client: Reling Zrt

To transform the outdated brand image into a modern and dynamic identity that resonates with the target audience, we revamped the logo, redesigned the website, and created a cohesive visual language that reflects the company's innovative approach. The rebranding resulted in increased brand recognition and a boost in customer engagement.

Portfolio 2: Client: Rural Ministry of Development Hungary: Secondary Market of Waste Management Project

We conducted extensive branding and market research, delving deep into understanding the target audience's attitudes, behaviors, and preferences related to waste management on the secondary market. Through thorough analysis and insights, we developed a comprehensive branding strategy that effectively communicated the project's goals and benefits to the Rural Ministry of Development, offering comprehensive solutions for future project implementations. The research findings served as a foundation for creating a compelling brand identity and implementing tailored marketing initiatives that resonated with the community, fostering positive engagement and encouraging sustainable waste management practices.

Portfolio 3: Client: Wasabi Wallet

Working closely with Wasabi Wallet, we undertook a comprehensive company rebranding effort to elevate their brand image in the competitive digital wallet market. In addition, I built a dynamic marketing team consisting of 15 talented individuals, and together, we developed a robust marketing, PR, and social media strategy. Our collaborative approach resulted in a successful brand transformation, increased brand awareness, and a strengthened online presence, positioning Wasabi Wallet as a trusted and innovative player in the industry.
